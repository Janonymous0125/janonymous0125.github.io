My Game Developing Story

I love playing games with family and friends, it pulls our relationships closer. When I was 8 years-old, my father and I would sit infront of the big and bonky computer playing Command & Conquer:Generals (Zero Hour) that was the first game Ive played with my dad. Soon after, My curiosity grew with my age. I wanted to build a game that has never been seen, a great open world multiplayer game where every player has their own story.

I started my research on the 8th of August 2024, learning Unreal Engine. I choose Unreal because it has a big community and if you need help with anything tutorials, online forums are very easy to find, you can ask online too if you can't find any helpful documentation. Due to the size of it's community there are a lot of people willing to help out.
