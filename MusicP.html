My Music Production Story

Music kind of followed me whole of my life. I started to learn piano when I was 7 years-old, drum when I was 9 years-old, violin when I was 10 years-old, guitar and bass when I was 13 years-old, and started DeeJaying when I was 16 years-old. Till now, Im still serving in my church as a drummer and a singer. I rarely but would go to some events to DJ if invited by the organizers.

Music production is one of ways I put my imagination and creativity into. I mainly uses FL Studio to compose or remix my musics and songs. For DeeJaying and mixing, Im using an old pioneer DDJ-400 and Rekordbox.
